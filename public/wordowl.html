WordOwl

The World Finder

WordOwl is word-finding tool and word game companion, capable of searching for words by starting letters, anagrams, letter quantities, contained strings and so much more. Whether you're looking for words containing 'panda', anagrams of 'integral' or words with three pairs of double letters, WordOwl has it covered. Users can even build their own search tools using the sophisticated Compound Search mode.

I decided to make WordOwl after becoming frustrated with the lack of available alternatives. Whilst you can find an ad-ridden online tool for most searches you'd want to perform, prior to WordOwl there were no solutions that combined all of these features into a single, elegant app. WordOwl aims to provide just such an app, and is available for free on the AppStore!

The Ultimate Word Finding Tool

WordOwl is packed with features, including 16 highly-customizable searching tools, an advanced Compound Search mode, a wealth of sorting options and a detailed user guide. After performing a search, the app helps you to narrow down your search further by displaying details such as scrabble scores, filtering options with a checklist mode, and exporting your results for use in other apps.

An Extensive Library of Tools

WordOwl's 16 core tools are powerful enough to handle almost any search imaginable. The tools range from basic functions such as searching for anagrams, starting letters or contained strings to truly advanced tools such as the Contains Quantities tool or the Matches Pattern tool. These tools are capable of performing complex searches such as 'words containing two pairs of double letters that start and end with the same letter', or 'words containing at least five Es but no more than two Ts'.

WordOwl also contains a selection of 'solver' tools, such as the Crossword Solver and the Wordle Solver which are designed specifically for your favourite word games and puzzles.

Unparalleled Flexibility with Compound Search

WordOwl's flagship feature is the Compound Search tool, an interface that allows users to build their own custom search functions by combining and inverting filters based the core built-in tools. This feature is what really sets WordOwl apart from competing apps, opening up searching possibilities previously only accessible using code.

Thorough Tool Documentation

Whilst WordOwl's tools are carefully designed to be user-friendly and easy to use, more advanced users will appreciate the detailed User Guide included within the app. The pages in the guide provide thorough documentation on all of WordOwl's tools and many of its other features.

Privacy Policy

WordOwl does not collect or store any user data or information except for search parameters and user preferences which are stored on your device. No third parties have access to your data or perform analytics. The app does not provide any services that may be used to identify or track you.
