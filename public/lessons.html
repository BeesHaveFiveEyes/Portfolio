Lessons

Invoicing Tool

Lessons is a tool designed to assist freelance tutors with scheduling lessons and managing their incomes. The app syncs with your iOS calendar to display your teaching schedule and automatically calculate your total hours, income and more. The app can even export its data ready to be copied directly into an invoice.

Tutoring Made Easy

Once linked with you chosen calendar, lessons provides a plethora of useful functions to support tutors:

  • A monthly dashboard shows your upcoming lessons and monthly totals including hours, total students and income.
  • Detailed views showing trends in your income, hours and student counts over time with helpful charts
  • Comprehensive preferences to allow for a range of contracts including 13-month contracts
  • The facility to export a month's lessons in invoice format for smoother and more reliable invoicing.

Privacy Policy

The app does not provide any services that may be used to identify or track you. No third parties have access to your data or perform anayltics. The only data stored by the Lessons app is the following, which is stored exclusively on-device:

  • A choice of iOS calendar
  • Salary details entered in the settings menu
  • User preferences
